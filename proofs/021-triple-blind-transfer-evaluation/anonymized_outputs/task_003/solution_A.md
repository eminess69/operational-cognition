# Solution A

Do not accept the green dashboard as current authority because its export predates the VIP change and the reported queue aging. Treat frontline ticket age and training eligibility as the immediate operational evidence.

Run a short containment cycle:

1. Freeze broad reassignment until the current ticket age export is generated.
2. Pull live counts by queue, age bucket, VIP marker, required skill, and owner.
3. Ask workforce to mark which agents are cleared for enterprise work today.
4. Move only trained agents or supervisor-approved shadow pairs into the enterprise queue.
5. Keep onboarding coverage above a named minimum so the fix claim does not create a second queue failure.
6. Recheck after one hour and record deltas.

Authority order is live ticket state, training eligibility, VIP list, then dashboard and product volume claims. Product's fix may reduce future arrivals, but it does not resolve aged open tickets until support verifies the trend.

Stop conditions are missing live export, no trained capacity, VIP mismatch, or onboarding coverage dropping below the minimum. The plan should report an interim rebalancing action, not a durable SLA recovery claim.
