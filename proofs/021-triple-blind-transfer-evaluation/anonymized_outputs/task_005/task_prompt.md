# Task 005: Security Exception Sunset Plan

A security exception expires Friday. The team must decide whether to block release, extend the exception, or ship with compensating controls. The evidence is mixed:

- The scanner baseline is 45 days old.
- The product owner says the patch breaks a partner workflow.
- Security wants the exception closed by the deadline.
- Audit needs evidence that the decision was risk accepted.
- Exploit signal is ambiguous and not yet tied to the affected component.
- A partner contract has a service commitment next week.

Produce a bounded sunset plan with authority ordering, evidence refresh, and risk branches.
