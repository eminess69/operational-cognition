## Replay Authority Implications

Replay can preserve operational sequence while losing authority lineage. Authority-aware replay requires enough provenance to recover what belief was active, where it came from, whether it was current, whether it was superseded, whether it was contradicted, and whether it was promoted, summarized, or recalled before use.
