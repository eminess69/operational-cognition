Visible-only solution. No pond-backed consult or candidate refs were used.

        ## Decision

        Do not commit to the quarter-end regional launch until assumptions are refreshed and rollback staffing is confirmed. Finance pressure is a planning input, not the authority for operational readiness.

        ## Authority Order

        2. Refreshed region risk measurement after customer onboarding.
        3. Dependency-team status and finance launch target.
        4. Copied go/no-go note only as a checklist seed, not as evidence.

        ## Plan

        1. Refresh support and customer-success inputs within one business day.
        2. Re-score regional risk using post-onboarding telemetry and open migration tickets.
        3. If rollback staff are available for one window, choose either a narrow canary in that window or delay.
        4. Record launch, delay, and partial-rollout alternatives with pass/fail gates.

        ## Uncertainty and Recovery

        The copied go/no-go note and stale register cannot justify launch. Recovery requires a rollback staffed window and a decision log that explains why any stale assumption was accepted or rejected.
